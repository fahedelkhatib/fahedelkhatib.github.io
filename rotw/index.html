Statistics on ReviewBrah's Report of The Week Ratings

Last updated: 02/04/20


Summary

This is a statistical analysis of YouTuber ReviewBrah's fast food ratings, called Running on Empty. Data is from a JSON api found at this github page, and includes many of ReviewBrah's fast food ratings from 2011 - 2018.

Average Score

First I had calculated an average score of all ratings marked Running On Empty:

Avg. Score = 7.6
Rating Count = 467

Average Score by Year

Next I calculated the average rating per year.

Year Average Rating Number of Ratings
2011 5.7 3
2012 8.5 1
2013 7.5 16
2014 7.5 79
2015 7.8 113
2016 7.5 155
2017 7.4 99
2018 7.5 51

Interestingly, the average score remained fairly stable, especially the prior 5 years, which are also the years with the most ratings in the dataset (approx. 96% of the total dataset).

Average Rating by Month and Week

After running yearly numbers, I ran weekly and monthly numbers, getting the average score per day of the week and per the month. One again, the numbers were very stable throughout the days of the week and the months.

Conclusion

Ratings appear very stable through time, especially over the past 5 years, and show no noticable monthly or weekly seasonality.
